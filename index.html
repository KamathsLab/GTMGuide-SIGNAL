The Problem

Your GTM Isn't Broken.
It's Leaking.

Most AI founders have a product that works — but a GTM that doesn't. And they have no idea which part is causing the damage.

Unpredictable Pipeline

Revenue exists but can't be forecasted. Deals close inconsistently and you can't explain why some months hit and others don't.

Wrong Customers Churning

You're closing deals — but the wrong ones. Poor ICP fit means high churn, low NRR, and a CS team fighting fires instead of driving expansion.

Fragmented Messaging

Your homepage says one thing, your sales deck says another, and your team is winging objection handling. No consistent narrative = no trust.

Blind Demand Gen Spend

Budget is allocated to channels based on gut feel, not attribution data. You're spending on demand gen but can't tell what's actually working.

The Scoring System

YES. PARTIAL. NO.

A weighted 3-tier scoring model. Each pillar normalises to a 0–100% score so every signal is fairly compared — regardless of question count.

YES
2 points
This is fully operational, documented, and consistently applied in your GTM motion.
PARTIAL
1 point
This exists in some form but is inconsistent, undocumented, or not fully operationalised.
NO
0 points
This is absent or so early-stage that it isn't contributing to your revenue engine.

The 6 Signals

What SIGNAL Stands For

Six diagnostic pillars — each covering a distinct layer of your revenue engine. Miss any one of them and the whole motion leaks.

S
Signal 01
Segment & ICP
5 Questions

Is your ICP validated, operationalised, and actively filtering bad-fit pipeline? Do you have an Anti-ICP? Are you tracking buying triggers?

I
Signal 02
Intent & Positioning
4 Questions

Do you have clear PMF evidence? Is your positioning anchor defined against your real alternatives — not just competitors on a G2 grid?

G
Signal 03
GTM Messaging
5 Questions

Does your homepage communicate value in 5 seconds? Is your narrative consistent across every GTM asset — from dark social to sales deck?

N
Signal 04
New Pipeline & Demand
5 Questions

Is your primary acquisition channel generating repeatable pipeline? Do you have a validated attribution model and a diversified demand gen mix?

A
Signal 05
Acquisition & Sales Motion
5 Questions

Is your sales motion (PLG, sales-led, hybrid) validated? Is your process documented with clear entry/exit criteria and stage-specific enablement?

L
Signal 06
Loyalty & Expansion
5 Questions

Are you delivering time-to-value in onboarding? Is your CS team equipped with health scores, expansion triggers, and NRR as a primary growth metric?
